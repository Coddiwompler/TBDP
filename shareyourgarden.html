How to Share Your Garden

Are You a Local Business with Some Dirt We Can Plant!?

One way in which local businesses can help our project is to donate a patch of land that we can plant together. Our wonderful volunteers would be happy to help you dig into that dirt, plant some of your purchased bulbs, and add your plot of earth to the colorful painting that we're trying to create for Belfast. Plus it gives your business a little bit of flair to help it really pop come springtime.


What to Expect When You Share Your Garden

  • We ask that you don't develop or destroy the shared space for at least 5 years so that we can really see how your space adds to the whole project
  • Try to fill out the form as much as possible so we can get a good idea of what the situation is
  • There's no size minimum or maximum, any little bit helps
  • After you get in touch, we will get back to you as soon as possible about if and when we can use your space
