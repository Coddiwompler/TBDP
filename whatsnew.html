The Latest News

It's that time of year! Daffodils are out all around town. It's time to enjoy them, pick them, smell them...AND order for next year! Check out where the empty holes are in your landscape and plan for fall planting. The deal is the same: 25$ for a bag of 100. Order as many as you want. Here's how:


Step 1...Contact: Email belfastdaffodilproject1@gmail.com.

  • Tell us your full name and how many bags you would like.

Step 2...Pay: Use one of the two following methods

  • Make a check out to The City of Belfast and send to Elisabeth Wolfe at 8 Bayview St, Belfast, Me 04915

    • OR

  • Pay through Venmo ( our ID is @daffodilproject )

**We will send a follow-up email in late-summer/early-autumn of when and where your bulbs can be picked up.


2021 Photo Competition

We love to document what an amazing difference a simple project like this can make, and we're sure you do too! Send us your best daffodil pictures from anywhere in Belfast (home garden, office window planter, or anywhere else they are showing their pretty little heads in our community), and you will be entered to win a free bag of bulbs this upcoming autumn! Simply email belfastdaffodilproject1@gmail.com with your name, the picture, and the place it was taken. The drawing for the winner will held on May 31st, and we'll notify the lucky duck via the email they used to send us their submission!


"Head Gardeners Wanted!"

It's simple: contact your neighbors, get their orders for bulbs, collect the orders when they're delivered in the fall, and then distribute! Easy! Contact Elisabeth Wolfe for more information - (207) 338-4783
