What's New!

The Latest News

The daffodils arrived! All 26,000 of them! (That will make for a combined total of 102,000 thus far!). They arrived October 1st and will be stored at the Belfast Boathouse. If you pre-ordered bulbs, please reach out to us about when would be best for you to pick up your order, or you can check out the calendar on the Belfast Daffodil Project website which will list possible pick up times and locations.

If you have ordered through a neighborhood “head gardener” they will get your bulbs to you. Also, if you forgot to order bulbs you can pick some up at 8 Bayview St in Belfast. They will be in a storage bin out front. Simply make out a check to The City of Belfast ($25 per bag) and stick it in the mailbox by the door. Any questions, call 207-338-4783, send an email, or check out the website. See you out in the dirt!
