How to Donate

You're so generous! Thank you for helping make our community more vibrant than ever.

Thanks for choosing to support us! Whether you're one of our community's great local businesses or just Bob and Sue from down the road, we have a few ways in which you can donate to The Belfast Daffodil Project:


Check

Make your check payable to: City of Belfast

and mail it to:

Elisabeth Wolfe
8 Bayview Street
Belfast, ME 04915

**Make sure to add a note telling us if you want to:

  1. Plant the daffodils yourself (add your contact information to the note and we'll let you know when pickup is available!)
    2. or
  2. Donate them to the city and we will plant them in one of our community gardens for you

Go Fund Me

For convenience purposes we have started a GoFundMe page which will allow you to quickly and easily help us along our way.

Venmo

You can also donate money to The Belfast Daffodil Project with your Venmo account by searching for the user @daffodilproject.
